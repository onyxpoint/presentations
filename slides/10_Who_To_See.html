My Recommendations

There are going to be a lot of excellent speakers at the conference but the following is a short list of folks that I've seen and enjoyed in the past

  • Henrik Lindberg
    • The primary author of the future/4.0 Puppet parser. Very much worth listening to for an insight into what's cutting edge.
  • Dawn Foster and Kara Sowles
    • The folks that run the community. Usually very interactive and want to move the community efforts forward.
  • Nan Liu
    • Previously worked for Puppet Labs and very informative on running Puppet within automated testing frameworks.
  • Daniele Sluijters
    • Creator of Puppetboard and good source of information on API interfacing with Puppet.
