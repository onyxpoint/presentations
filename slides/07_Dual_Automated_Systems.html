Dual Automation Systems

  • Manage your base config with Puppet
  • Manage your specific application with something else
    • In this case, we're using Ansible
  • Pros
    • Everyone gets to pick their own tool
  • Cons
    • More difficult to monitor, integrate, and test
    • Internal approval groups may have a hard time with multiple solutions performing the same tasks
    • May end up with dueling automation
    • End up with split talent pools
